Pigment Violet Products

Qualitron Chemicals is one of the leading exporters of Pigment Violet. We have expertise and network chain specially developed for our special products Pigment Violet 3 (Pigment Violet 772) Pigment Violet 27 (Pigment Violet 777).

Specifications for Pigment Violet Products

Pigment Violet 3 (Violet 772)

Pigment Violet 3 is very bluish, clean shade violet. Soluble in cold water and hot water, soluble in ethanol for purple.

Pigment Violet 3 (Violet 772)
Mass Tone of Pigment Violet 3 (772)
Tint Tone of Pigment Violet 3 (772)

Applications of Pigment Violet 3 (Violet 772)

Recommended for water-based inks and solvent inks. Suggested for offset inks, water-based coating and textile printing. Ink Applications: Offset, polyamide, vinyl, PU, NC, HDPE, screen, water base

Pigment Violet 27 (Violet 777)

Pigment Violet 27 (Violet 777) Pigment Violet 27 is a bright, blue shade violet with outstanding brilliant color purity and tinting strength.

Pigment Violet 27 (Violet 777)
Mass Tone of Pigment Violet 27 (777)
Tint Tone of Pigment Violet 27 (777)Tint Shade Pigment Red 57:1 (Rubine Ton

Applications of Pigment Violet 27 (777)

Pigment Violet 27 is primarily recommended for water-based printing inks. Possible other uses may include oil inks.
