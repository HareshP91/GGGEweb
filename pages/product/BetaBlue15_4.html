Phthalocyanine Pigment Blue 15:4 (Pigment Beta Blue)

Pigment Blue 15:4 is a bright, greenish blue synthetic blue pigment from the group of Phthalocyanine Dyes. Phthalocyanine Pigment Blue 15: 4 is also a beta form of CPC Blue crude. This dry pigment offers industrial coatings market all the desirable properties of phthalocyanines. The light fastness, tinting strength and stability allows to be used in inks, coatings and many plastics.
