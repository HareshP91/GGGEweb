Pigment Red

Qualitron Chemicals is one of the leading exporters of Pigment Red Series. We have expertise and network chain specially developed for our products Pigment Red 53:1 – Lake Red & Pigment Red 57:1 – Rubine Toner.

Specifications for Pigment Red Products

Pigment Red 53:1 (Lake Red)

Pigment Red 53:1 (Lake Red)
Pigment Red 53:1 – Lake Red
Pigment Red 57:1 – Rubine Tone

Pigment Red 53:1 (Lake Red) is a bright yellowish red pigment of excellent brightness and tinting strength. Pigment Lake Red is the bluest shade Lake Red C in our range.

Slightly soluble in water and ethanol, not soluble in acetone and benzene.

Applications of Pigment Red 53:1 (Lake Red)

Pigment Lake Red is primarily recommended for use in solvent packaging ink systems such as nitrocellulose and polyamide inks, water based and UV printing inks.

Pigment Red 57:1 (Rubine Toner)
Full Shade of Pigment Red 57:1 (Rubine Toner)
Tint Shade Pigment Red 57:1 (Rubine Ton

Pigment Red 57:1 (Rubine Toner) is a reddish synthetic azo dye. It has the appearance of a red powder and magenta when printed.

It is slightly soluble in hot water, insoluble in cold water, and insoluble in ethanol.

Applications of Pigment Red 57:1 (Rubine Toner)

Pigment Red 57:1 (RubineToner) is used to dye plastics, paints, printing inks, and for textile printing. It is normally used as a standard magenta in the Three and Four-color printing processes.
